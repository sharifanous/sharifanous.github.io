• ROOM ROLL-UP
  • proposes a prefabricated, combinatorial design & construction system for ADU's (accessory dwelling units).

    This system strategically employs developable curvilinear and planar geometries to operate in a way that allows the building enclosure to be:

    Fabricated flat in a warehouse

    Rolled up like a carpet

    Shipped and unrolled quickly on site.

     Princeton University
    M.Arch Thesis Project, Spring 2020
